About the Game

Where's My Lamb? is a relaxing grid-based puzzle game. In a colorful N×N farm, each row, column, and color region hides a certain number of sheep (normal mode: 1 per group, advanced mode: 2 per group). Sheep cannot be adjacent to each other (including diagonally). Use logic to find all the sheep!

How to Play

  • Single tap a cell: Toggle ✕ (exclude) or ⚑ (assume) marks
  • Double tap a cell: Place a sheep 🐑
  • Flag mode: Switch to ⚑ marks to highlight potential sheep locations
  • Hint system: Up to 3 hints per puzzle

Rules

  • Each row, column, and color region contains a fixed number of sheep
  • Sheep cannot be adjacent (including diagonally)
  • Wrong placements cost one life; you have 3 chances total

FAQ

Q: The game is laggy. What should I do?
A: Try closing other background apps to free up device memory. If the issue persists, try restarting the game.
Q: Does the game require an internet connection?
A: No. The core gameplay is fully offline.
Q: Will I lose my progress?
A: Game progress is saved in the device's local storage. Uninstalling the app will erase your progress, so please avoid clearing app data unnecessarily.
Q: How do I switch languages?
A: You can switch between Chinese and English in the Settings page.
