Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: The tuner cannot detect my guitar sound. What should I do?

Please make sure:

  • You have granted microphone permission to the App.
  • The surrounding environment is relatively quiet, minimizing background noise.
  • The guitar playing sound is clear enough. Try playing closer to the device microphone.
  • No other app on your device is currently using the microphone.

Q2: The tuning results seem inaccurate. What can I do?

The App uses a high-precision pitch detection algorithm and generally provides accurate tuning results. If you notice a significant deviation, please try:

  • Using the App in a quiet environment.
  • Plucking one string at a time, avoiding playing multiple strings simultaneously.
  • Checking whether your device microphone is working properly.

Q3: The metronome has no sound or the sound is abnormal?

Please check:

  • Whether the media volume on your device is turned up.
  • Whether your device is in silent mode.
  • If connected to Bluetooth earphones, confirm that the audio output device is selected correctly.

Q4: The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?

Please try the following:

  • Close the app and reopen it.
  • Check if there is an app update available.
  • Restart your device.
  • If the problem persists, please send your device model, system version, and a description of the issue to our technical support email. We will resolve it as soon as possible.

Q5: How can I report a bug or submit a suggestion?

We greatly value your feedback. You can submit bug reports or improvement suggestions via our technical support email ssyu8950210@163.com. To help us process your request more efficiently, please include:

  • Your device model and operating system version
  • App version number (available on the settings page)
  • A detailed description of the issue and steps to reproduce it
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if available
