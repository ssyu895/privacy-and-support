App Features
CostKeeper helps you track your purchases and calculate their daily value. Key features include:
  • Track items with name, price, category, and purchase date
  • Calculate daily value based on usage duration
  • Set expiration dates for items
  • View spending statistics and category distribution
  • Add photos to your items for better organization
  • All data stored locally for privacy and security
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Will my data be synced across devices?
A: Currently, CostKeeper uses local storage only. Your data is stored on your device and does not sync automatically.
Q: What happens if I uninstall the App?
A: All your data will be lost if you uninstall the App. Please make sure to back up any important information before uninstalling.
Q: How do I add photos to my items?
A: When adding or editing an item, tap the image area to either take a new photo or select one from your photo library. The photo will be stored locally on your device.
Q: Can I change the category of an item after creation?
A: Yes, you can edit any item including its category, name, price, and other details at any time by tapping on the item in your list.
Q: Does CostKeeper collect any personal data?
A: No, CostKeeper does not collect any personal data. All your information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.
