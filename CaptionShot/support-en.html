Frequently Asked Questions

?
How do I generate App Store screenshots?

Go to the "Screenshot" tab, tap "Select from Gallery" to upload your screenshots, then tap "Export PNG" to generate promotional images with device mockups.

?
How do I translate metadata?

Go to the "Translate" tab, enter your app information, select target languages and translation mode (offline/online), then tap "Start Translation".

?
What's the difference between offline and online translation?

Offline translation uses iOS 18+ built-in translation framework — all processing is done on-device with no internet required. Online translation uses AI translation services for higher quality results but requires an internet connection.

?
Where are my images saved?

Generated screenshots are saved to your system photo album. All images and metadata within the App are stored locally on your device only.

?
Which device sizes are supported?

We support iPhone 6.9" to 6.1" and iPad 12.9"/13" — covering all mainstream App Store screenshot sizes.

Quick Start Guide

1
Select Images

Tap "Select from Gallery" on the Screenshot tab to choose your screenshots. Supports multi-select, up to 10 images.

2
Choose Device

Tap the "Size" button to select your target device. The preview will automatically adapt to the correct aspect ratio.

3
Edit & Customize

Tap the "Edit" button to open the editor, where you can add text, adjust backgrounds, apply templates, and more.

4
Export

Tap "Export PNG" to generate promotional images with the starry night theme device mockup, automatically saved to your album.
