Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Introduction

CaptionShot (the "App") respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects minimal information, solely to provide core functionality:

  • Screenshot Images: Images you select from your photo library are processed entirely on your device to generate App Store previews with device mockups. We do not upload your images to any server.
  • App Metadata: Your app name, subtitle, description, and other text are stored locally on your device. When using online translation, text content is translated using an AI translation service, but is not collected or stored by us.
  • Usage Data: The App does not collect any analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Processing your screenshots locally to generate promotional images
  • Sending text to an online translation service (based on your selection)
  • Improving the App's user experience and functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All images and metadata are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • The App uses iOS system-provided secure storage mechanisms to save your data.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

In online translation mode, the App uses the following third-party services:

  • Online Translation Service: Used for online translation. Your text content is sent to a third-party translation service for processing.

In offline translation mode, all processing is done entirely on-device, no internet connection required, and no data is sent to external services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • As required by law
  • To protect our rights or property

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete all images and metadata within the App at any time
  • You can choose offline translation to avoid any data transmission
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified within the App. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
