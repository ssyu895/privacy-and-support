Help & Support

Welcome to succuSnap! Here you'll find frequently asked questions and contact information to help you get the most out of the App.

FAQ
Q: How do I add a new plant?
Tap the "+" button at the bottom right of the Home page. Fill in the plant name, upload a photo, set the purchase price and date, then save.
Q: How do I record my plant's growth changes?
Go to the plant's detail page and tap the "+" button at the bottom right. You can take a photo, select care actions (water, fertilize, etc.), and add notes.
Q: How do I compare before and after photos?
On the plant's timeline page, tap the "Compare" button at the bottom, select 2-6 photos to generate a comparison collage. The comparison image can be saved to your album.
Q: How do I mark a plant as deceased/sold/given away?
Tap the "Memorial" option in the top-right menu on the plant detail page and select the appropriate status. The plant will be moved to the "Memorial" page.
Q: Will my data be lost?
All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing data will result in data loss. Please back up your device data regularly.
