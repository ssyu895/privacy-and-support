Privacy Policy

succuSnap ("the App") respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: May 2026

Information Collection & Use

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information.

Specifically:

  • Plant Data: Plant names, photos, prices, care records, and other data you add are stored locally on your device. We do not access or upload this data.
  • Photo Permissions: Camera and album permissions are requested only when you actively take or upload plant photos. These photos are used solely for in-app display and comparison features, and are never uploaded to any server.
  • App Settings: Your preferences are stored only in the device's local storage.
Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera To capture photos of your plants' growth
  • Photo Library To upload plant photos and save comparison images

These permissions are requested only when needed. You can revoke them at any time in your system settings.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear all data at any time from the App's Settings page.
