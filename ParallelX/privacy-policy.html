Privacy Policy

ParallelX - Dual Clone APP
Last updated: June 27, 2026

ParallelX - Dual Clone APP is designed to help you create and manage app clones on your device. We process app and clone information locally by default and do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Process

Depending on how you use the app, ParallelX - Dual Clone APP may process the following information:

  • Installed app information, such as app name, package name, version, and app icon, so we can show apps that may be cloned.
  • Clone and virtual space records, such as clone name, icon, package name, space number, hidden state, and shortcut settings.
  • Device-related identifiers used inside the virtual environment, such as virtual device identifiers generated or managed for cloned apps.
  • App settings, such as language, theme preference, recent-use records, and customization choices.
  • Technical diagnostic information if the app crashes or if you actively contact us for support.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only for app functionality and support, including:

  • Displaying installed apps that can be cloned.
  • Creating, launching, hiding, repairing, uninstalling, or managing cloned apps.
  • Saving your app customization, theme, language, and virtual space preferences.
  • Creating desktop shortcuts when requested by you.
  • Diagnosing compatibility, permission, and crash issues.
  • Improving app stability and user experience.

3. Local Processing and Storage

Most information used by ParallelX - Dual Clone APP is processed and stored locally on your device. This includes your clone records, customization settings, virtual space records, and app preferences. We do not upload your installed app list or cloned app content by default.

4. Permissions

The app may request permissions only when needed for related features, such as:

  • Reading installed apps to show the app list.
  • Creating shortcuts when you request a desktop shortcut.
  • Accessing photos or media when you choose a custom icon from your gallery.
  • Other permissions required by cloned apps to run inside the virtual environment.

You can manage permissions in your device system settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may include third-party components or SDKs needed for app functionality, diagnostics, advertising, analytics, billing, or distribution. These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies. We aim to use only the data necessary for the relevant feature.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in limited cases:

  • When you explicitly send diagnostic details or support information to us.
  • When required to comply with applicable laws, legal processes, or enforceable government requests.
  • When necessary to protect the security, rights, or safety of users, the app, or others.

7. Data Retention

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, uninstall the app, or remove the related clone or virtual space. Support emails and diagnostic information you choose to send may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and improve the app.

8. Children's Privacy

ParallelX - Dual Clone APP is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke permissions through your device settings.
  • You can delete clones, clear clone data, or clear the app's data from system settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data controlled by ParallelX - Dual Clone APP.
  • You can contact us to ask privacy-related questions.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and may provide additional notice where appropriate.
