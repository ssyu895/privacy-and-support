KittyVision Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

KittyVision (referred to as "the App" or "we") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

KittyVision does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, IMEI, MAC address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other sensitive data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

All game data (such as catch records) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Network Access

KittyVision is a fully offline application. It does not require an internet connection to function. The App never connects to the internet or transmits data in the background.

3. Third-Party Services

KittyVision does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. The App contains no advertisements.

4. Data Storage

The App stores only the following data locally on your device:

  • Game catch records (scores, catch counts, etc.)
  • Volume and language preference settings

This data is entirely under your control. Deleting the App or clearing its data will permanently remove all stored information. We have no access to this data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
