Quick Start Guide

Getting started with KittyVision is easy:

  • Open the App and pick a scene your cat will love (Butterflies, Fish, Birds, Mouse, or Bugs)
  • Place your device in front of your cat and let them watch and tap the screen
  • Sound effects provide feedback when your cat catches something, encouraging interaction
  • Tap the back button (top-left) to view your cat's catch results

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My cat isn't interested in the screen. What can I do? Try switching to a different scene—every cat has its preference! Some love butterflies, while others prefer fish or birds. You can also turn up the volume, as sound effects often help grab your cat's attention.
Q: Why is there no sound? Please check if your device is in silent mode, and make sure the volume is turned up in the App's settings. KittyVision's volume is independent from the system volume and needs to be adjusted within the App.
Q: Does the App require an internet connection? No. KittyVision runs completely offline. No internet connection is needed. All content is built into the App.
Q: Does the App collect my personal data? No. KittyVision is fully offline and does not collect any personal information. No ads, no tracking. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.
